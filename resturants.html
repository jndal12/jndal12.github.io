  • Wah's Kitchen

There are many great resturants to go to while you are in Fresno. The best Chinnese food you can get would be Wah'sThey serve a lot of food for one plate and the quality is great.

Another good place to eat would be Castillo's Mexican Resturant

My other choice would be going to Dog House . They have amazing tri-tip sandwitches and other items on the menu are just as good.

My final resturant recomendation is Sakanaya. They put on quite a show when they cook but their food is so flavorful, it's hard to watch when you are too focused on eating.
