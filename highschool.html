I went to high school at San Joaquin Memorial. I graduated with a class of 128 students. It had a total of 565 students at the time I went there. It was considered to be a college prep school but I do appose that phrase. We are also known as SJM and if you click the 'SJM', then you will be directed to the school's website. It was a good place to grow up and make friends. I met a couple of great mentors there, such as my baseball coach and my physics teacher. Good things must come to an end to hopefully better times.
